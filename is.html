Intake Specialist

This is a living document and is subject to change. Last updated: 6 June 2026
Role Purpose

To ensure only guideline compliant submissions progress through the pipeline and assess possible thematic fits.

IS Responsibilities
  • Ensuring submissions are in the correct Google Drive folder.
  • Reviewing submissions for guideline compliance.
  • Assessing potential theme alignments for assigned submissions.
  • Sending appropriate form letter rejections for guideline violations.
  • Routing appropriate submissions to the Intake Lead for redistribution.
  • Overseeing the work produced by assigned intern.

Interns: While you will do many of these tasks, your assigned mentor must sign off before any actions are finalized.

Why It Matters

The Intake Specialist ensures that the publishing process begins with clarity, fairness, and respect for editorial labor. By owning submission readiness and mentoring new interns, this role safeguards both the integrity of the pipeline and the culture of the organization.
