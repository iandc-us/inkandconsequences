Intake Editor

This is a living document and is subject to change. Last updated: 26 June 2026
Role Purpose

The Intake Editor conducts the first editorial review of assigned submissions, evaluating each manuscript against the publication's editorial standards before making a recommendation.

IE Responsibilities
  • Conducting blind, independent readings of submissions assigned by the Senior Intake Editor
  • Reviewing submissions for guideline compliance.
  • Evaluating submissions for craft, execution, clarity, and editorial suitability
  • Recording a clear editorial recommendation in accordance with established decision categories
  • Assigning the final theme based on fit and availability
  • Routing appropriate submissions to the Senior Intake Editor for final decision or rerouting.
  • Overseeing the work produced by assigned intern.

Interns: While you will do many of these tasks, your assigned mentor must sign off before any actions are finalized.

Why It Matters

The Intake Editor ensures that editorial judgment is applied rigorously, fairly, and consistently at the point of intake. By contributing independent evaluation within a structured decision model, this role safeguards both editorial integrity and institutional trust.
