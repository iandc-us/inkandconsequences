IE Workflow

This is a living document and is subject to change. Last updated: 26 June 2026
The Resources You Will Use

Open these first.

  • The internal Theme Guide found here.
  • This page in the IKB.
  • The submission tracker which can be found here.
  • The Duosuma submission manager accessed through Duotrope here.
  • The reading guide located here.
Step 1

Access your Duotrope dashboard and select the oldest assigned submission.

Step 2

Copy the submission ID.

Step 3

Search for the submission using Ctrl+F and entering the submission ID. Open the file through the tracker in column Y.

Step 4

Open the Word document in Google Docs.

Step 5

Update the tracker in the first open IE column with your name and the status of the submission.

Step 6

Complete each section of the Reading guide and update status in tracker to C.

Once tracker is updated, you can reassign the submission in Duosuma to your Senior Intake Editor and move on to the next assigned submission.
